How It Works

Each node in the murmuration follows three rules:

  • Alignment: Steer toward average heading of neighbors
  • Cohesion: Move toward average position of neighbors
  • Separation: Avoid crowding neighbors

When a probe (attack) is detected:

  • The nearest node transforms (changes color/size)
  • The pattern is disrupted but reforms elegantly
  • Art is generated from the attack vector
  • The entire flock learns and adapts
